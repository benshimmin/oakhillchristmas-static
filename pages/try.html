Watch Online

Oakhill Village Christingle Service is online this year and we will be uploading it here on Christmas Eve for you to watch at home. All of our school children will receive a candle and some red ribbon to take home, just add an orange and a few sweets and make your own Christingle with Reverend Richard.

Children’s Work

If you have children and would like to see them engaging with church then we have the following child-friendly events happening:

Christingle Service (online).

Children's Nativity Service in person at 11am on Sunday December 20th in Oakhill Methodist Church

Nativity Stones - all our school children will be given a bag and a Nativity Story booklet so that they can collect their Nativity Stones and re-tell the story of Jesus for themselves.

Owing to Covid restrictions we are doing less at the moment than normal, so please contact us if nothing listed here is quite right but you are interested to hear about other events and activities in the future.
